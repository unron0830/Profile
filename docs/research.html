博士論文 Ph.D. Dissertation

多尺度山崩變動之監測與估算：結合地基雷達、遙測影像與生成對抗網路之整合應用
Monitoring and Estimation of Multi-Scale Landslide Changes: Integrated Applications of Ground-Based Radar, Remote Sensing Imagery, and Generative Adversarial Networks
國立成功大學 資源工程學系，博士論文（2026）
Ph.D. Dissertation, Department of Resources Engineering, National Cheng Kung University (2026)

本研究整合地基合成孔徑雷達（GBSAR）、多時相衛星遙測影像與生成式深度學習模型， 建立山崩變形監測、地形差異（DoD）推估與崩塌量體估算之完整流程， 並驗證其於實際災害案例與治理情境中的可行性， 強調研究成果由方法發展延伸至系統實作與決策支援。

This dissertation integrates ground-based SAR (GBSAR), multi-temporal satellite imagery, and generative deep learning models to establish a unified workflow for landslide deformation monitoring, DEM of Difference (DoD) estimation, and landslide volume quantification. The study emphasizes deployable solutions bridging methodology, system implementation, and decision support in real-world hazard scenarios.

碩士論文 Master’s Thesis

1961–1990 臺灣 30 年月均溫與月雨量空間推估——導入地形特徵
Physiographically Sensitive Mapping of Climatological Temperature and Precipitation in Taiwan (1961–1990)
國立臺灣師範大學 地理學系，碩士論文（2016）
M.S. Thesis, Department of Geography, National Taiwan Normal University (2016)
論文全文（NTNU Repository）→ Full thesis (NTNU Repository) →

本研究參考 PRISM（獨立坡向指數高程迴歸模式）， 將測站氣候資料推估為 300 公尺解析度之網格化溫度與雨量資料， 並透過交互驗證顯示其相較於克利金法與距離反比權重法具有較佳推估表現， 奠定後續空間推估與遙測分析之研究基礎。

This thesis applied the PRISM model to interpolate station-based temperature and precipitation data into 300 m gridded climate surfaces. Cross-validation results demonstrated improved performance over kriging and inverse distance weighting, forming a solid foundation for later work in spatial interpolation and remote sensing analysis.
